Our Mission

Welcome to our transportation website. We are passionate about different modes of transportation around the world.

Our mission is to showcase beautiful images of various transportation methods including boats, planes, and cable cars.

This website was created as part of the FNB App Academy learning project.

Our Team

We are a group of enthusiastic developers learning web development and creating simple HTML pages.

Our team focuses on creating clean, accessible HTML without relying on CSS or other styling methods.

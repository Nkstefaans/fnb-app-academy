About Boats

This is a page dedicated to boats as a mode of transportation.

Boats have been used for transportation for thousands of years and remain an important way to travel across water.

Boat Image

A beautiful boat sailing on the water.

Boat Facts

  • Boats are watercraft used for travel, recreation, fishing, and transportation.
  • They come in many sizes, from small dinghies to large ships.
  • Boats can be powered by sails, engines, or human power (rowing/paddling).

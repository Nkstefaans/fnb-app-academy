The Avengers Initiative

The Avengers are a fictional team of superheroes that appears in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team made its debut in The Avengers #1 (cover-dated Sept. 1963), created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist/co-plotter Jack Kirby.

Known as "Earth's Mightiest Heroes", the Avengers originally consisted of Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk, Ant-Man, and the Wasp. Captain America was discovered trapped in ice in issue #4, and joined the group after they revived him.

Our Mission

The Avengers were assembled to combat threats that no single superhero could handle alone. Their mission is to protect Earth from both domestic and extraterrestrial threats.

This website showcases some of the most iconic members of the Avengers team, providing information about their backgrounds and abilities.
